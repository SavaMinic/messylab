Messy Lab was developed as a part of Miloš Anđelković's Master's thesis at the Department of Software Engineering, School of Electrical Engineering, University of Belgrade.

The name "Messy Lab" comes from the fact that assembly programs tend to get, well, messy. Is is also an anagram of the word "assembly".
